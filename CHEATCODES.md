# Cheatcodes

Defender of Egril includes cheatcodes for testing and fun. Access them by clicking on the coins display during gameplay.

## Available Cheatcodes

### Money Cheatcodes
Add 1000 coins to your balance:
- `moneybags`
- `1000coins`
- `cash`

### Spawn Enemy Cheatcode
Spawn enemies of any type at any level:

**Format:** `spawn <type> [level]`

**Examples:**
- `spawn goblin` - spawns a level 1 goblin
- `spawn ogre 3` - spawns a level 3 ogre (with 2x health)
- `spawn wizard 5` - spawns a level 5 evil wizard (with 3x health)

**Available Enemy Types:**
- `goblin` - Fast but weak (20 HP, speed 2)
- `ork` or `orc` - Slow but tough (40 HP, speed 1)
- `ogre` - Very tough (80 HP, speed 1)
- `skeleton` - Fast undead (15 HP, speed 2)
- `wizard` or `evilwizard` - Magical attacker (30 HP, speed 1)
- `witch` - Dark magic wielder (25 HP, speed 2)

**Level Scaling:**
- Level 1: Base health
- Level 2: 1.5x health
- Level 3: 2.0x health
- Level 4: 2.5x health
- And so on...

### Jump to Level Cheatcode
Jump directly to any level in the game:

**Format:** `level <N>` or `jumplevel <N>`

**Examples:**
- `level 1` - jumps to level 1 (The First Wave)
- `level 3` - jumps to level 3 (The Ork Invasion)
- `jumplevel 5` - jumps to level 5 (The Final Stand)

This will automatically unlock all levels up to and including the target level.

## Usage
1. Start any level in the game
2. Click on the coins display (top-left corner)
3. Enter one of the cheatcodes above
4. Click "Apply" to activate the cheatcode

## Notes
- Cheatcodes are case-insensitive
- Enemy spawn requires a free spawn position
- Level numbers must be between 1 and 5
- Enemy health scales by 50% per level above 1
