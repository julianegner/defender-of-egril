DEFEND EGRIL - THE LAST STAND ON THE MEADOWS

The evil warlord Ewhad has rallied the Hordes of Gleid Thyae and set his sights on Egril's peaceful meadows. Only your tactical skill and a well-placed line of defenses can save the land.

Defender of Egril is a turn-based tower defense strategy game. Careful planning, smart upgrades, and precise targeting lead to victory.

--- GAMEPLAY ---

* Place and upgrade towers before and during each wave
* Manually choose which enemy each tower attacks
* Manage action points: every tower has a limited number of attacks per turn
* Earn gold by defeating enemies and invest it in new towers or upgrades
* Survive all enemy waves to unlock the next level on the world map

--- TOWERS ---

Spike Tower - Melee, affordable and reliable
Bow Tower - Long-range archery for distant threats
Spear Tower - Medium range with piercing power
Wizard Tower - Area-of-effect fireballs hitting multiple enemies at once
Alchemy Tower - Acid attacks with lasting damage
Ballista Tower - Extreme-range siege weapon with a minimum firing range
Dwarven Mine - Generates gold coins through mining actions

--- YOUR ENEMIES ---

Face ever-growing hordes: swift Goblins, armored Orks, massive Ogres, Skeletons, evil Wizards who summon demons, Red Witches who disable towers, Green Witches who heal allies - and at the end, the mighty Ewhad.

--- FEATURES ---

* Turn-based strategy
* World map with unlockable levels and increasing difficulty
* Use experience points to unlock skills and spells
* Save and load your game progress at any time
* Pan and zoom the battlefield with an auto-appearing minimap
* Fully localized: English, German, Spanish, French, and Italian
* Level Editor in the desktop version
* An optional remote account enables server-side saving and switching between devices with the same game progress.
